GATE ACADEMY



Over the past 14 years, the team has developed the course curriculum and teaching methodology for the GATE examination. This has also taken a tangible shape in the development of preparatory programs for entrance and aptitude tests for various Public/Government Sector jobs. The design, development and delivery of each of the programs offered at The GATE ACADEMY incorporates the best teaching practices relevant to the tests.. Academy is a notable online teaching channel on YouTube that was created in February 2017 with the goal of imparting technical education to the students and to help the GATE aspirants pursue their dreams.

The founder of the GATE Academy, Umesh Dhande Sir , himself had to struggle a lot during his GATE examination period due to lack of coaching facilities and expert guidance. A great teacher and an excellent motivator, he is a name which is very popular among the engineering students.
